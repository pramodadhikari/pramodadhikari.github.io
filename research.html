Plain Language Summary of my PhD

My PhD research explored the impacts of atmospheric aerosols (natural and anthropogenic) on precipitation and cloud dynamics over the Himalayas. Using satellite data, reanalysis datasets, and high-resolution (3 km) WRF-Chem simulations, we examined aerosol effects across daily to interannual scales...
